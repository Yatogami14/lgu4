System Flow and Explanation for Digital Health & Safety Inspection Platform

1. User Roles and Authentication:
- Users access the system via a central login page (main_login.php).
- Upon login, users are redirected based on their role:
  - Admin and Super Admin: Redirected to admin dashboard.
  - Inspector: Redirected to inspector dashboard.
  - Business Owner and Community User: Redirected to community user landing page.
- Role-based access control ensures users can only access permitted areas.

2. Dashboards and Functionalities:
- Admin Dashboard:
  - Displays overall system statistics: total businesses, inspections, inspectors, and active violations.
  - Shows upcoming inspections, recent activities, and community reports awaiting action.
  - Provides visual charts for inspection statuses and compliance trends.
- Business Owner Dashboard:
  - Shows businesses owned by the user, total inspections, open violations, and average compliance score.
  - Allows business owners to request new inspections.
  - Displays recent inspections and compliance trends.
- Inspector Dashboard:
  - Lists inspections assigned to the inspector with status breakdown (completed, in progress, scheduled).
  - Shows upcoming inspections with links to start inspection forms.
- Community Dashboard:
  - Provides public access to business compliance data.
  - Allows searching businesses by name, address, or type.
  - Displays statistics such as total businesses, average compliance, and high-risk businesses.

3. Inspection Lifecycle:
- Inspections can be scheduled by admins or requested by business owners.
- Inspectors conduct inspections using AI-assisted tools.
- Inspection reports are generated and violations tracked.
- Compliance scores are calculated and trends monitored.
- Community members can view public compliance scores to promote transparency.

4. Additional Features:
- Centralized login with "Remember Me" functionality.
- Role-based navigation and access control.
- Notification and reporting systems integrated into dashboards.
- AI-powered analysis for inspection notes and media.
